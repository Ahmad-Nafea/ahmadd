Tourist places in Aqaba

  1. Aqaba Fortress

    2. s Aqaba Castle or Mamluk Castle is one of the most important tourist places in Aqaba, which is located on the shore of the Gulf of Aqaba. The castle was established in the fourteenth century during the Mamluk era, on the ruins of a previous Crusader castle. Aqaba Castle was used as a station for pilgrims heading to Makkah, and the castle had a prominent role during the First World War (1917 AD), as it was from there that the spark of the Great Arab Revolt broke out, and it also gained a special place after the movie Lawrence of Arabia was filmed in it, which increased its tourism importance.

  2. Wadi Rum

    3. Wadi Rum belongs to the Aqaba Governorate in southern Jordan, and it is 70 kilometers away from the city, and it is one of the best tourist places in Aqaba, as the place is distinguished by its picturesque nature in which there is no type of pollution, although it has gone through many climate changes, and among the scenes that The place is distinguished by the charming sunset view, and tourists can camp in the place for several days to enjoy the clear skies and air free of pollutants.

  4. Sherif al-Hussein bin Ali Mosque

    5. The Sharif Al-Hussein Bin Ali Mosque was established in 1975, and it was renovated in 2011 AD. This mosque is considered one of the most important tourist attractions in Aqaba, as it is characterized by charming architectural designs made of white stone, in addition to the towering lighthouse.
